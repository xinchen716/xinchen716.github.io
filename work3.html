ZOMIA

Interactive performance of dance capture and 3d animation.

ZOMIA served as a sanctuary for mountain tribes escaping lowland control.

The Zomia civilization embodies mutual interdependence rather than strict domination.

Choreography based on Southeast Asian instruments and the use of motion capture and avatar design, realizing the reality and virtual co-presentation.
