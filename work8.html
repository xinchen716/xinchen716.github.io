Algae Feast

"Algae Feast", a mutual aid "body farm". Algae overpopulation causes eutrophication of water bodies, based on the characteristics of algae that can easily build a symbiotic relationship with living organisms, we think about whether edible algae can be cultivated on human bodies, envision a new mutual-help diet model in the future to alert people to the environmental problems of water bodies.

Traditional dietary patterns have caused excessive phosphorus in water bodies, eutrophication, overgrowth of algae, and a freshwater crisis, which is one of the causes of future food shortages.Based on the fact that algae are easy to survive, rich in a variety of nutrients needed by human beings, and easy to build a symbiotic relationship with living organisms, we wondered whether it is possible to cultivate edible algae on the human body, and create a self-sufficient body farm through the heat produced by the human body itself and photosynthesis of the algae.
