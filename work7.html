Mirror

"Mirror" is an immersive audio-visual interactive installation that centres around the concept of the mirror as a metaphor for self-perception and identity. At its core, the work invites viewers to question whether our identities in the digital era are authentic representations of who we truly are or merely virtual constructs shaped by data, algorithms, and the digital environment. Throughout history, mirrors have been potent symbols of self-reflection, serving as tools for individuals to examine and understand themselves.
