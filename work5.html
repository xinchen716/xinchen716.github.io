不熄 BUXI

This project draws inspiration from my hometown’s Sea Lantern Festival. It also considers recent occurrences of harmful red tide and advancements in biological light technology.

Water represents the essence of all life forms, while biological light symbolizes the soul of the ceremony, signifying the harmonious relationship between human and nature. By employing ritual actions and 3D modeling, I hope to convey my vision for the future blessing ceremony and to draw attention to the problems of the marine environment.
