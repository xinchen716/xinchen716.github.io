STUPIGOOD

STUPIGOOD explores the jester as a symbol of freedom of expression and resistance in the digital age. Drawing from traditional comedy and heterogeneous bodies, I analyzed their physicality, movements, and satirical techniques. The project addresses modern speech restrictions and reimagines the jester as a vital figure for future cultural movements, using humor to challenge authority and inspire awareness.
