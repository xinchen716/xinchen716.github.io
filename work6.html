Wake Up

WAKE UP is a project inspired by my recovery from a ligament injury, during which I reflected on the fast-paced, detached lifestyles prevalent in modern urban China. Rooted in Levinasâ€™ philosophy of Existence and Existents and experimental research, the project explores physical actions like breathing, gazing, eating, and working as methods of rediscovering self-awareness and emotional perception.

I categorized these into three types: eating, outdoor experiences, and self-exploration. Using clay as a medium, I recorded tactile sensations and collected data to design three future products that symbolize daily awakening rituals. This work encourages people to reconnect with their present state and regain a sense of self-existence in an materialistic world.
